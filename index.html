Learn how to dream better
Sleep Position: Changing your sleep positions can effect your dreams, tests say that sleep on your left compared to the right can lead to nightmares. Sleeping on your stomach can lead to smothering or unable to move dreams. The pressure on your body brings more feeling to your nerves which can trigger these more powerful sensations. Try sleeping on the opposite side to inprove your dream quality.
